In 2015, Accela led a coalition of civic technology companies and other stakeholders in the development of the first ever shared data specification for building permit data. This new standard is being implemented in cities across the country and will provide new and powerful insights into the operation of cities and the changing character of communities. This site provides a sample of some of the data that has been published by different jurisdictions in the new Building and Land Development Specification (BLDS) data format.
